<h1 style="text-align:center">Welcome to Hantz Group</h1><p>Hantz Group is a Michigan-based financial company that works with individuals and businesses to help them achieve their financial goals. For the individual, we offer a Family Office of services tailored to your needs and goals, ranging from budgeting and banking to insurance, investments and tax services. We build a team of experts around you to work in collaboration and help coordinate superior outcomes. </p>
<h2 style="text-align:center;">Why use a Family Office?</h2>
<p>By taking our holistic and comprehensive approach, we reduce the stress of navigating a complex financial situation and consolidate services to save time for our clients so they can focus on what is most important for their families. We believe in providing a high level of service by deploying specialists in each financial area, and assembling those specialists into a collaborative team.</p>
