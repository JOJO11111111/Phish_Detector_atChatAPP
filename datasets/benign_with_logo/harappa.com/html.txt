<div class="views-field views-field-field-question-image"> <div class="field-content"><a href="/content/current-research-indus-archaeology"><img alt="" height="220" src="https://harappa.com/sites/default/files/styles/medium/public/images/indus-sites-excavations.jpg?itok=M7bvQ-Df" typeof="foaf:Image" width="195"/></a></div> </div>
<div class="views-field views-field-body"> <div class="field-content"><p>A recent publication by the South Asia Research Group at Kansai University in Japan that includes detailed essays covering a general picture of Indus research today (by Akinori Uesugi), Indus civilization in the Ghaggar Basin (Vivek Dangi) , Indus archaeology in Gujarat (Rajesh S.V.), steatite style variations between Gujarat and the Ghaggar-Hakra Basin (Gregg Jamison) and Indus copper wares (Takekazu Nagae).</p></div> </div>
