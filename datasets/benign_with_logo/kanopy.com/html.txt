<div class="opener">
<h3 class="header">Frequently Asked Questions</h3>
</div>
<div class="accordion">
<div class="title active">
                What is Kanopy?
                <i class="icon"></i>
</div>
<div class="content active">
<p class="body transition visible">Kanopy is the best video streaming service for quality, thoughtful entertainment. Find movies, documentaries, foreign films, classic cinema, independent films and educational videos that inspire, enrich and entertain. We partner with public libraries and universities to bring you an ad-free experience that can be enjoyed on your TV, mobile phone, tablets and online.</p>
</div>
<div class="title">
                How is Kanopy free for me?
                <i class="icon"></i>
</div>
<div class="content">
<p class="body transition hidden">Just as your library buys books to add to their collection for you to borrow, your library offers a variety of digital resources too - Kanopy is one of their more popular digital resources. Kanopy does not charge you because the public library or university covers all associated costs, allowing you to watch for free with no ads.</p>
</div>
<div class="title">
                Why do I need a library card or university login to sign up?
                <i class="icon"></i>
</div>
<div class="content">
<p class="body transition hidden">Kanopy is only available through libraries, so you need to be a member of a participating library to start watching Kanopy.</p>
<p class="body transition hidden">If you are a member of a participating public library, you can start watching by signing up with a valid public library card number and PIN/password. </p>
<p class="body transition hidden">If you are a currently registered student or faculty member of a participating college university, you can start watching by signing up with a valid university login.</p>
</div>
<div class="title">
                How do I know if my library or university offers Kanopy?
                <i class="icon"></i>
</div>
<div class="content">
<p class="body transition hidden">For public libraries, check to see if your library has made Kanopy available <a href="/signup/find/publiclibrary">here</a>. Type in the name of your library, or enter your zip code to display nearby libraries that have made Kanopy available. </p>
<p class="body transition hidden">For universities, check to see if your library has made Kanopy available <a href="/signup/find/university">here</a>. Type in the name of your university to see if they have made Kanopy available.</p>
<p class="body transition hidden">Don’t see your library listed? Click on “Can’t find your library” and fill out the form to request that your library makes Kanopy available.</p>
</div>
<div class="title">
                How do I get started?
                <i class="icon"></i>
</div>
<div class="content">
<p class="body transition hidden">Getting started is easy! <a href="/signup">Click here</a> to find your library and enter your library card information or university login when prompted. Follow the simple instructions to create your Kanopy account using your email address or Facebook/Google login and then start watching.</p>
</div>
<div class="title">
                What is Kanopy Kids?
                <i class="icon"></i>
</div>
<div class="content">
<p class="body transition hidden">Kanopy Kids is our collection of movies and shows for kids available in public libraries only. Your public library offers unlimited plays in Kanopy Kids, so your kids are free to explore enriching, educational and entertaining films and TV series. <a href="/kids">Click here</a> to check out Kanopy Kids!</p>
</div>
</div>
