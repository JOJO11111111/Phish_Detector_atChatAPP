<p>Effective January 1, 2020, filing fees for real estate deed documents, UCCs, certain types of liens, and plats are changing. House Bill 288, which modifies O.C.G.A. § 15-6-77, institutes a “flat” or “predictable” filing fee structure. <a href="/learn/house-bill-288">Read More</a></p>
