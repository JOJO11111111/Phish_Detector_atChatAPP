<h3 id="klcp">What is the Kali Linux Certified Professional (KLCP)?</h3>
<p style="text-align: Justify;">The <strong>Kali Linux Certified Professional</strong> (KLCP) is a professional certification that testifies to ones <strong>knowledge</strong> and <strong>fluency</strong> in using the Kali Linux penetration testing distribution. KLCP holders can demonstrate an in depth understanding and utilization of the Kali Linux operating system. Individuals with this certification have the skills, knowledge and abilities to put Kali Linux to use as advanced power users, capable of creating highly customized and secure deployments. In addition, the KLCP certification provides foundational knowledge for any information security professional – allowing them to use it as a solid base in their career. The certification exam can be scheduled and purchased through <a href="http://vue.com/kali/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">VUE Testing centers</a> around the world.</p>

<h3>How do I prepare for the KLCP?</h3>
<p style="text-align: justify;">The body of knowledge that the KLCP relies upon can be found in the <strong>Kali Linux Revealed Book</strong> (<a href="https://www.amazon.com/Kali-Linux-Revealed-Penetration-Distribution/dp/0997615605" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">hardcopy</a> and <a href="/topic/abstract/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" title="Kali Linux Revealed Book">online version</a>). To prepare yourself for the KLCP certification you must read, understand and assimilate the topics and materials of the whole Kali book. The online version of this book includes additional challenges which drive through these topics via hands-on exercises – make sure to complete these exercises, while understanding exactly what you are doing, down to the individual commands. For extra assurance, familiarize yourself with the documents found in the <strong><a href="https://docs.kali.org/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Kali Documentation</a></strong> site. Once you are fluent with this material, you can consider scheduling your exam with <strong><a href="http://vue.com/kali/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Pearson VUE</a></strong>.</p>
